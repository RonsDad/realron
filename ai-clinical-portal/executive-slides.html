Lessons Learned from Early Adopters

  • 💡
    Start with high-impact use cases

    Focus on areas with clear ROI and measurable outcomes

  • 💡
    Invest heavily in training

    User competence directly correlates with system effectiveness

  • 💡
    Maintain human oversight

    AI augments but doesn't replace clinical judgment

  • 💡
    Plan for continuous improvement

    AI models require ongoing refinement and validation
